About us

Our vision was born out of our international experience that we observed that buying second-hand cars in other countries was easier, transparent and could be done 100% online. In contrast, in Spain, what should be a magical and simple process, for many was complex and with the ever-present feeling that there would be unexpected surprises. We are a team of people with experience in the world of motoring, technology and marketing who have built a new concept of buying and selling cars in Spain based on transparency, simplicity and trust. We have designed a 100% digital customer experience that allows you to buy a car from your couch and receive it in less than 48 hours at your doorstep, and with a unique return guarantee of 15 days or 1.000 km in which if you do not fall in love with the car, you get your money back in 24 hours.

Simplicity: 100% online process, which makes buying a car as easy and fun as driving it. Transparency: giving the maximum information to customers, and the best treatment because the experience of our customers is the most important thing for us. Trust: We reinvent the second hand concept to "good hand cars", thanks to our review, the most complete of the market in 250 points; the Clicars quality seal with guaranteed mileage, zero structural damage, mechanics 100% guaranteed and revised 360º and 1 year free warranty and return in 24h.
